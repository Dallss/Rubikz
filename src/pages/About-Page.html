RUBIKZ is an interactive 3D Rubik's Cube simulator. It allows you to visualize and manipulate a Rubik's Cube directly in your web browser. You can rotate the cube, move its layers, and experiment with solving it.

Whether you're a beginner trying to learn how to solve a Rubik's Cube or an expert looking to practice your skills, RUBIKZ provides a convenient and engaging way to interact with this classic puzzle.
